The problem

Why institutions are left behind

Platforms like Molecule and ResearchHub have validated research tokenisation—but their crypto rails are incompatible with UK institutional procurement, risk appetite, and regulatory frameworks. ORS solves this with GBP-native settlement and institutional IP protection built in.

Crypto rails

  • ✕Token & stablecoin settlement
  • ✕Outside HEI procurement rules
  • ✕Unclear IP & governance
  • ✕High regulatory uncertainty

ORS — GBP-native

  • ✓Pound sterling settlement
  • ✓Fits UK procurement frameworks
  • ✓Institutional IP protection
  • ✓Compliance-first design
How it works

Three steps from proposal to funded research

  1. Researchers submit proposals

    Early-stage research projects are structured into transparent, fundable opportunities.

  2. Funders back research in GBP

    Capital flows in pounds sterling with familiar settlement — no tokens, no exchanges, no volatility.

  3. Institutions retain IP governance

    Ownership and oversight stay where they belong — with the institution.

    No blockchain. Full transparency. Institutional control.

Who it's for

For researchers
